About Me

I'm Juliet Aneke — a Chartered Accountant, Full-Stack Developer in training, and Artificial Intelligence enthusiast.

My journey started in accounting, where I developed strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Today, I am expanding my expertise by learning modern web development and artificial intelligence, preparing myself for a future at the intersection of finance and technology.

Born and raised in Nigeria, I come from Udi in Enugu State and am the sixth child in a family of seven. These experiences taught me adaptability, hard work, and the importance of building meaningful relationships.

My mission is simple: to leverage technology, innovation, and analytical thinking to create solutions that make a lasting impact. Every project I build is another step toward that goal.
